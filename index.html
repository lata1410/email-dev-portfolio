lautaro´s e-mail dev portfolio 📧

Archive of mails and newsletters that I developed for clients on a freelance basis and working for Intaface. My task is to layout the mail through code and make it responsive both on different screen sizes and on different mail clients (from the oldest to the newest).
For a long time the development of email campaigns was a very cumbersome task, but luckily the development of frameworks such as MJML and the help of testers makes it much easier. Different mail clients have different ways of rendering HTML files and that makes it sometimes complex. That is why it is important that the design of the pieces prior to development takes their limitations into account and maintains a simple and readable form.
~ It's incredible that after so much time emails are such an important tool, right? ~

Web technologies that I use:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • MJML
  • EPS (Email Preview Service)
  • File Transfer Protocol (FTP)
